Innovation Platform · 2024 — present

Desh Ka Insurance (DKI)

Conceptualised, pitched, funded, shipped — a first-of-its-kind PWA for rural microinsurance.

The brief

BFSI distribution couldn't reach rural India at unit economics that made microinsurance work. The traditional channel was broken for the segment that needed cover most.

The move

I conceptualised DKI — a Progressive Web App enabling rural POS agents to onboard, train, and sell microinsurance across India. I built the business case, pitched it internally, and secured funding from the Dai-ichi Life Innovation Fund — making DKI one of the first innovation-funded digital distribution platforms in Indian insurance.

What it produced
  • Secured $300K — $100K PoC + $200K seed — from Dai-ichi Life Innovation Fund
  • Awarded the DINO Credential — blockchain-verified innovation recognition from Dai-ichi Life Holdings
  • First-of-its-kind PWA microinsurance distribution platform in Indian BFSI
  • Built training, onboarding and sales modules for non-traditional rural POS agents
  • Established a repeatable innovation-funding playbook for future SUD Life initiatives

Platform & MarTech · 2022 — present

SUD Life — full digital rebuild

Joined with one mandate: build a digital marketing function that didn't exist. Three years in, here's what runs.

The brief

Star Union Dai-ichi Life had no in-house digital team, no MarTech infrastructure, no SEO programme, no real lead generation pipeline. Brand awareness in a saturated category. A static site. Zero playbook.

The move

Built it all. End-to-end Strapi (Headless) website rebuild — WCAG 2.1 AA compliant. Full MarTech stack from scratch — CleverTap, GA4, Looker Studio, LeadSquared. SEO programme with 170+ hyperlocal pages. 166 Google Business Listings. Lead pipeline with sub-2-minute lead-to-call latency. Rs. 7 Cr budget under full IRDAI compliance.

What it produced
  • Rebuilt the website on Strapi — WCAG 2.1 AA, modular components, audit-ready
  • 727K users with 35.2% traffic growth driven by on-page and hyperlocal SEO
  • Average keyword rank from 40.5 to 11.4 — 202 keywords ranking #1
  • 17M MarTech events tracked across CleverTap, GA4, Looker, LeadSquared
  • 30K qualified leads delivered in FY26 — lead-to-call latency < 2 minutes
  • Instagram +900% (3K → 30.2K), Facebook +90% (50K → 97K+)
  • Rs. 7 Cr annual marketing budget, full IRDAI regulatory compliance

A passive customer-service app turned into an engagement engine — through retention-first product marketing.

You Matter was a wellness benefit nobody opened twice. Installs were vanity. DAU/MAU was honest — and brutal.

The move

What it produced
  • Scaled to 170K installs through coordinated feature-marketing campaigns
  • DAU/MAU lifted by 35% via retention-first lifecycle CRM
  • Reframed the app from cost centre to brand-affinity channel
  • Established CRM playbooks now used across SUD Life customer touchpoints
